Study Habits

In a world where education has received significant investment, the high returns can be perceived directly or indirectly based on achievement, progression, and placement. Most of the time, students have not had to manage their time efficiently because they are bright and rarely challenged in school. By developing effective study habits, students may be able to cut down on the number of hours spent studying, leaving more time for other things in their life. "Most of us spend too much time on what is urgent, and not enough time on what is important." - Steven Covey
